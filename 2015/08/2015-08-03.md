## Tools

### Ask Sublime to ignore files

```javascript
"file_exclude_patterns": ["*.pyc","*.pyo","*.exe","*.dll","*.obj","*.o","*.a",
                          "*.lib","*.so","*.dylib","*.ncb","*.sdf","*.suo","*.pdb",
                          "*.idb",".DS_Store","*.class","*.psd",
                          "*.db","*.sublime-workspace"],
"folder_exclude_patterns": [".svn",".git",".hg","CVS","node_modules"]
```
